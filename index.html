FAQ

FAQ

How long do the results last?
The effects are strongest for up to 8 hours and can be reapplied as needed. The product works best when applied to clean and dry skin. Allow 10 minutes for Rapid Reduction Eye Cream to set before applying anything over it.
How long does the product last if left unopened?
Gracemoor Beauty Rapid Reduction Eye Cream has a 2-year shelf life if unopened. Once a tube is opened it should be used within a few weeks. Always replace cap pin side down to maintain freshness between uses.
How much solution should I use for one application?
We suggest starting with about half the size of a pea, which should be enough to apply under each eye. However, everyone’s skin is different, so if you notice the skin tightening effects are too strong, simply reduce the amount of formula to a level that works best for you.
What is the best time of day to apply Gracemoor Beauty Rapid Reduction Eye Cream?
The product can be used any time of day. Just make sure to wash and dry your skin thoroughly before application.
Why am I getting a white residue?
If you are getting a white residue around the application area it usually indicates that you have used too much product. Excess residue can be easily removed by gently tapping the area with a damp cloth or cotton ball. Avoid rubbing as this may break the formulas bond with your skin.
Can you apply makeup over this product?
Avoid applying makeup directly on top of areas you have used Rapid Reduction Eye Cream. If you must use makeup on top of the cream, stick to oil-free products and apply very gently and sparingly over the cream after it has set.
Can I use a moisturizer with this product?
Rapid Reduction Eye Cream works best on its own. Try using your moisturizer in the evening to help rehydrate your skin. If you must use a moisturizer during the day, make sure it is oil-free, and apply it about 20 minutes before the cream. This gives your skin time to properly absorb your moisturizer so it will not impact the skin-tightening benefits of the cream.
Does this work on other parts of the face and neck?
Yes, Rapid Reduction Eye Cream can be used to help visibly tighten and smooth all areas of the face and neck.
Can this product be used on the sagging upper eye lid?
Caution should always be taken when using any beauty product around the eyes. That area is sensitive and we do not recommend applying Rapid Reduction Eye Cream on your eyelid.
Does Gracemoor Beauty Rapid Reduction Eye Cream work on darker skin?
Yes, with proper application, this product works on all skin tones.
What is Acetyl Hexapeptide-8?
Acetyl Hexapeptide-8 is a peptide clinically studied to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles with daily use.
Does the product contain Retinol?
Gracemoor Beauty Rapid Reduction Eye Cream does not contain Retinol. It uses the power of silicate minerals to form a tightening layer on the surface of the skin, reducing the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles and under-eye bags in minutes.
Do you practice cruelty-free? I do not want to purchase products that are animal tested.
Gracemoor Beauty products are never tested on animals.
Is Gracemoor Beauty Rapid Reduction Eye Cream FDA approved?
This is not a food or a drug. All of the ingredients in the Gracemoor Beauty Rapid Reduction Eye Cream are considered safe for use in cosmetic and skincare products.
Is the product absorbed by the skin or does it sit on the surface?
Gracemoor Beauty Rapid Reduction Eye Cream works by creating an invisible layer on the surface of your skin that temporarily tightens the appearance of under-eye bags and wrinkles.
